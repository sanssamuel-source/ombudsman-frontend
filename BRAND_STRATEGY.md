## üîç Market Research & Context

### Problem Space Analysis

**Current State of Public Service Accountability in Sierra Leone:**

- Paper-based complaint systems requiring physical presence in Freetown
- No tracking mechanism for complaint status
- Rural citizens excluded due to distance and cost barriers
- No data analytics to identify systemic corruption
- Citizens distrust government processes due to lack of transparency

**Market Gap:**
| Traditional System | Digital Opportunity |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| Manual, paper-based | Digital, accessible 24/7 |
| Freetown-only access | Nationwide coverage |
| No status updates | Real-time tracking + SMS |
| No pattern detection | AI-powered analytics |
| Excludes feature phones | USSD support (\*920#) |

### Target Audience Segments

#### Primary: Rural Citizens (60% of population)

- **Demographics**: Ages 25-65, farmers, traders, laborers
- **Location**: Bo, Kenema, Makeni, Port Loko districts
- **Tech**: Basic feature phones, limited internet
- **Pain Points**: Distance to Freetown, transportation costs, illiteracy
- **Needs**: Simple USSD interface, SMS confirmations, voice support (future)

#### Secondary: Urban Professionals

- **Demographics**: Ages 22-45, students, office workers, entrepreneurs
- **Location**: Freetown, Bo City, Kenema City
- **Tech**: Smartphones with internet
- **Pain Points**: Time constraints, want evidence of submission
- **Needs**: Web/mobile app, photo upload, email notifications

#### Tertiary: Government Officials (Ombudsman Office)

- **Demographics**: Ages 35-60, civil servants, investigators
- **Location**: Freetown HQ, regional offices
- **Tech**: Desktop computers, tablets
- **Needs**: Dashboard analytics, case management, reporting tools

### Competitive Analysis

**Direct Competitors:** None (first digital ombudsman portal in Sierra Leone)

**Indirect Alternatives:**

- Social media complaints (Facebook, WhatsApp) - No official response
- 119 Emergency line - Not designed for corruption complaints
- Direct ministry contact - Often ignored
- Traditional media - Slow, limited reach

**Our Competitive Advantages:**

1. ‚úÖ Multi-channel access (web, SMS, USSD)
2. ‚úÖ Official government backing
3. ‚úÖ NIN verification for credibility
4. ‚úÖ Data-driven hotspot identification
5. ‚úÖ Already deployed and functional

**Do:** Use simple language that even low-literacy citizens understand.

```

‚ùå "Please specify your geographical jurisdiction"
```

#### 2. **Supportive & Encouraging**

```
‚úÖ "You're taking an important step for transparency"
‚ùå "This complaint will be processed according to statutory guidelines"

```

**Do:** Treat every citizen complaint with equal seriousness.

```
‚úÖ "Thank you for trusting us with your complaint"
```
