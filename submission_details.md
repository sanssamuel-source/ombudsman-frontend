### Short Description

**OmbudsLink** is a secure, accessible digital platform designed to democratize access to justice in Sierra Leone. It empowers citizens to anonymously report public service malpractice and track their complaints in real-time via web and SMS. By bridging the gap between the public and the Ombudsman's office, OmbudsLink ensures transparency, accountability, and efficient case resolution, transforming how the government listens to its people.
