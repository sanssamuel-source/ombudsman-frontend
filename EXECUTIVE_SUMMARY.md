## The Problem

Sierra Leone's public service accountability system is broken. Citizens who witness corruption or maladministration face impossible barriers:

- Must travel hours to Freetown to file complaints
- No tracking mechanism exists
- Paper-based system creates a "black hole" where complaints disappear
- 60% of the population (rural areas) is effectively excluded
- Ombudsman office cannot identify systemic corruption patterns

**Impact**: Trust in government erodes. Corruption goes unchecked. Citizens feel voiceless.

---

## Our Solution

The **Ombudsman Digital Portal** is a multi-channel platform that makes accountability accessible to every Sierra Leonean:

### Three Ways to Access

1. **Web Portal** - Full-featured interface for smartphone/computer users
2. **SMS** - Text complaints without internet
3. **USSD (\*920#)** - Works on basic feature phones with no data

### Core Features

- Real-time tracking with unique reference IDs
- Automated SMS status updates
- NIN verification for credibility
- Evidence upload (photos/documents)
- Admin dashboard with hotspot analytics
- Complete audit trails for transparency
